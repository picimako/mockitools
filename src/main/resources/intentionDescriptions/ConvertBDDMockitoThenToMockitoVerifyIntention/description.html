Converts BDDMockito.then().should() call chains to the Mockito.verify() approach.

The intention is available on BDDMockito.then() when it is followed by should() and a method call on the mock object.

It doesn't support the conversion of InOrder verification.

Conversion is possible only when the Enforce conventions inspection is disabled, or it doesn't enforce org.mockito.BDDMockito.
