Converts Mockito.verify() call chains either to BDDMockito.then().should() or InOrder.verify() based on the user's choice.

The intention is available on Mockito.verify() when it is followed by a method call on the mock object.

Conversion to BDDMockito.then().should() is available only when the Enforce conventions inspection is disabled, or it doesn't enforce org.mockito.Mockito.
