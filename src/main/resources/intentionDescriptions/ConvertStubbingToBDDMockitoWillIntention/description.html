Converts stubbing call chains to the BDDMockito.will*().given() approach.

The intention is available on static will*() methods of org.mockito.BDDMockito when

  • the result would not be the same as from what approach the user wants to convert, and
  • the from approach is Mockito.when().then*(), or
  • the from approach is BDDMockito.given().will*(), or
  • the from approach is Mockito.do*().when(), and the chain ends with .when(mock).doSomething();
