Converts stubbing call chains to the Mockito.do*().when() approach.

The intention is available on static do*() methods of org.mockito.Mockito when

  • the result would not be the same as from what approach the user wants to convert, and
  • the from approach is Mockito.when().then*() and there is no then() call in the chain, or
  • the from approach is BDDMockito.given().will*() and there is no will() call in the chain, or
  • the from approach is BDDMockito.will*().given() and there is no will() call in the chain, and the chain ends with .given(mock).doSomething();

The reason for excluding chains containing then() or will() calls is that they don't have a matching method in the Mockito.do*().when() approach.
