Converts stubbing call chains to the Mockito.when().then*() approach.

The intention is available on Mockito.when() when

  • the result would not be the same as from what approach the user wants to convert, and
  • the from approach is Mockito.do*().when() and there is no doNothing() call in the chain, and the chain ends with .when(mock).doSomething();, or
  • the from approach is BDDMockito.given().will*(), or
  • the from approach is BDDMockito.will*().given() and there is no willDoNothing() call in the chain, and the chain ends with .given(mock).doSomething();

The reason for excluding chains containing doNothing() or willDoNothing() calls is that they don't have a matching method in the Mockito.when().then*() approach.

Conversion from BDDMockito.given().will*() and BDDMockito.will*().given() approaches is possible only when the Enforce conventions inspection is disabled, or it doesn't enforce org.mockito.BDDMockito.
