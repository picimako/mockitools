Reports multiple consecutive calls to *Return() methods, so that they may be merged into a single call.

org.mockito.Mockito, org.mockito.BDDMockito and org.mockito.MockedStatic based stubbings are supported, including calls to doReturn(), thenReturn() and willReturn().

If there are multiple sections of consecutive calls within the same call chain, they are reported separately for better notification, and can be merged separately.
