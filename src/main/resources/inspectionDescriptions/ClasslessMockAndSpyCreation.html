This inspection reports arguments passed into Mockito.mock() and Mockito.spy() which are designed to determine the mock type based on the variable's type they are assigned to, and not by the type passed into them.

For example: 

    
MockObject mockWithAnswer = Mockito.mock(Answers.CALLS_REAL_METHODS, someOtherObject);
MockObject spied = Mockito.spy(new MockObject(), new MockObject());
