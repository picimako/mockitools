Reports usages of isNull(Class), isNotNull(Class) and notNull(Class) matchers which are deprecated since Mockito v2, and are removed in v4.

Accordingly, it also provides a quick fix to replace them with their non-parameterized variants.

The inspection is applied only when the Mockito version included on the module classpath of the currently inspected file is 2.x or 3.x.

Examples:


//from:
Mockito.when(mock.method(ArgumentMatchers.isNull(String.class))).thenReturn(10);
Mockito.when(mock.method(ArgumentMatchers.isNotNull(String.class))).thenReturn(10);
Mockito.when(mock.method(ArgumentMatchers.notNull(String.class))).thenReturn(10);
//to:
Mockito.when(mock.method(ArgumentMatchers.isNull())).thenReturn(10);
Mockito.when(mock.method(ArgumentMatchers.isNotNull())).thenReturn(10);
Mockito.when(mock.method(ArgumentMatchers.notNull())).thenReturn(10);

Furthermore, if the matcher is referenced via org.mockito.Matchers, it is also replaced with the org.mockito.ArgumentMatchers qualifier.
